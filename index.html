01 Logo System

The
Ariyak

The Ariyak logo is the primary brand asset. The triangular A-mark represents precision, ascent, and directional ambition. The geometric wordmark is bold, structured, and unmistakable.

02 Clearspace

Always maintain a minimum clearspace of "X" — the cap height of the wordmark — around all edges of the logo. No text, graphics, or other elements should intrude on this zone.

X clearspace X clearspace
03 Logo Rules

Do's & Don'ts

✓   Do
✓

Use the approved navy, black, or white background variants only.

✓

Maintain the required clearspace around the logo at all times.

✓

Scale the logo proportionally — always lock aspect ratio when resizing.

✓

Use the provided SVG or high-resolution files for all applications.

✓

On light backgrounds, use the reversed (dark) logo variant.

✕   Don't
✕

Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo in any direction.

✕

Do not rotate or flip the logo mark or wordmark.

✕

Do not apply unauthorised colours, gradients, or textures to the logo.

✕

Do not separate the A-mark from the wordmark without permission.

✕

Do not place the logo on busy, low-contrast, or patterned backgrounds.
