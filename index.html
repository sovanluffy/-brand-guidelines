01Logo System

The Ariyak Mark

The Ariyak logo consists of a triangular A-mark and a geometric wordmark. Four approved colour variants ensure correct display across any background. Never alter, recolour, or reconfigure these approved versions.

01 — Navy Background
02 — Black Background
03 — Monochrome Blue
04 — White / Reversed
02Clearspace

Logo Clearspace

Maintain a minimum clearspace equal to the cap-height "X" of the wordmark around all four sides of the logo. No text, graphics, or page edges should enter this protected zone.

X clearspace X clearspace
03Logo Rules

Do's & Don'ts

✓  Do
✓

Use only the four approved logo variants on their intended backgrounds.

✓

Maintain the required clearspace around the logo at all times.

✓

Scale the logo proportionally — always lock the aspect ratio.

✓

Use provided SVG or high-resolution files for all applications.

✓

On light backgrounds, use the reversed (navy) logo variant.

✕  Don't
✕

Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo in any direction.

✕

Do not rotate or flip the A-mark or the wordmark.

✕

Do not apply unapproved colours, gradients, or textures to the logo.

✕

Do not separate the A-mark from the wordmark without approval.

✕

Do not place the logo on busy, low-contrast, or patterned backgrounds.
